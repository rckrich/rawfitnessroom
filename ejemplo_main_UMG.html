FAQ.

When does the project launch?
The registration will be open soon for a limited time, please ensure you enter promptly. Announcements and important information on the minting date will be made on the Unicorn Motorcycle Gang Discord server or by following along @UMGbar on twitter.
What are NFTs?
NFTs are tokens that we can use to represent ownership of unique items. They let us tokenize things like art, collectibles, even real estate. In UMG case you'll receive collectible digital art, beer design voting rights, UMG beers purchase rights and exclusive interactions at the UMG Bar.
NFTs can only have one official owner at a time, and they're secured by the Ethereum blockchain - no one can modify the record of ownership or copy/paste a new NFT into existence.
What is the Blockchain?
A blockchain is a public database that is updated and shared across many computers in a network.
"Block" refers to data and state being stored in consecutive groups known as "blocks". If you send ETH to someone else, the transaction data needs to be added to a block to be successful.
"Chain" refers to the fact that each block cryptographically references its parent. In other words, blocks get chained together. The data in a block cannot change without changing all subsequent blocks, which would require the consensus of the entire network.
What is an Ethereum Wallet?
Ethereum wallets are applications that let you interact with your Ethereum account. Think of it like an internet banking app, without the bank. Your wallet lets you read your balance, send transactions and connect to applications. Want to learn more? Go to ethereum.org
What wallet is supported by UMG?
Metamask is the supported wallet of the UMG website. Metamask is available as a browser extension and as a mobile app, MetaMask equips you with a key vault, secure login, token wallet, and token exchange. Everything you need to manage your digital assets. Want to learn more? Go to metamask.io
What are the benefits from owning a UMG NFT?

Will the desigmed beer actually go into production?
Yes! Once a beer is designed by the UMG members, it will be produced in a specialized brewery in Yucatan following the highest quality standards.
I'm a UMG NFT holder. What can I do with the UMG-designed beer?
Once you get it, it's up to you. You can collect it, use it as a food ingredient (to marinate roast meat), trade it for any other good or take it to your friends party; It's up to you! Life is simple: it's about motorcycles, beer, rock, freedom and unicorns.
